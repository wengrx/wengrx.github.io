Biography

I am an NLP researcher and algorithm engineer at Alibaba DAMO Acadamy.

My research interests include Machine Translation, Natural Language Generation and Deep Learning. Currently, I focus on data-augmentation for NMT and cross-lingual representation learning.

We are recruiting research interns and full-time employees in machine translation, text generation, cross-lingual pre-training and other multi-lingual tasks. If you are interested in, please feel free to contact me.
